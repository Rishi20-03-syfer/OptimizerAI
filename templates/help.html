Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to the Multimodal AI Agent Help Center. Here you'll find information on how to use our AI agent effectively, troubleshoot common issues, and get answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Use

  1. Choose your input method (text, image, document, or voice).
  2. Provide your query or data in the selected input field.
  3. Click the "Submit" button to process your request.
  4. Wait for the AI to generate a response.
  5. Review the output in the designated area.

FAQ

Q: What types of input does the AI agent accept?
A: Our AI agent can process text, images, documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT), and voice inputs.
Q: How accurate is the AI's response?
A: While our AI strives for high accuracy, results may vary depending on the complexity of the input and the specific task. We recommend reviewing the output and using human judgment when necessary.
Q: Can I use the AI agent for commercial purposes?
A: Usage rights depend on your subscription type. Please refer to our terms of service or contact our sales team for more information on commercial usage.
Q: How is my data handled and protected?
A: We take data privacy seriously. All inputs are processed securely and are not stored permanently unless explicitly requested. For more details, please review our privacy policy.

Troubleshooting

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies if you experience persistent issues.
  • Check that your input adheres to the specified format and file types.
  • If problems persist, try using a different browser or device.
